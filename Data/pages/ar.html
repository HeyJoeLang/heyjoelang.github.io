Inside Rodin's Hands


A groundbreaking augmented reality exhibit at the Cantor Museum, blurring the lines between art, anatomy, and technology.

Using image-based tracking, the exhibit offers visitors a unique 'x-ray view' into Agustus Rodin's sculptures.

I programmed the app, collaborating with a medical artist to seamlessly integrate visuals and AR tools.

News report on 'Inside Rodin's Hands'

Avatar Medic


Training non-medical persons to administer disater relief intervention with IBM Watson toolset

I programmed the application.

IFTF Future 50: Hologram Dance Hall


For IFTF's Future 50 event, guests were scanned to create 3D models. That model is then animated to dance for an event music video.

I developed this pipeline from conception to execution, including scanning people, rigging their scans, adding the animations, and generating the music video.

Wallabe Sky


A project for Neumatic Digital, an augmented reality view of a customizable furniture for their client. Cross-platform running on Hololens, ARKit, and WebGL.

I developed the application for ARKit and Hololens
